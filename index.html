Services

AI systems we build

Production-grade computer vision pipelines and AI tooling — built end-to-end, deployed on your infrastructure.

01

Real-Time Object Detection

A robust, end-to-end pipeline that performs real-time detection across multiple live camera feeds at scale.

  • Instant alerts on rule-based or AI-driven events — crowd gathering, restricted-zone entry, and more.
  • Built for large-scale surveillance: hundreds of feeds in parallel.
  • GPU-optimised for minimal latency in production deployments.
02

Prompt-Based Visual Search

Detect any object described in natural language — on live feeds, with no custom dataset or retraining required.

  • Describe what you want to find: "person in red hoodie," "yellow helmet," "blue forklift."
  • Works instantly on live feeds — just type and detect.
  • No annotation, no training, no model fine-tuning needed.
03

Chatbot for Your Data

A conversational AI assistant that connects directly to your visual data — ask questions in plain English and get evidence-backed answers.

  • Query video archives, image datasets, or live feeds through natural conversation.
  • Every answer comes with timestamped clips and frame extracts as evidence.
  • Integrates with Slack, Teams, or your existing tools via REST API.
04

Face Recognition

High-accuracy facial recognition for access control, attendance, watchlist alerts, and identity verification — running on your own infrastructure.

  • Real-time identification across live camera feeds with sub-second response.
  • Privacy-first: all face embeddings and matching stay on your servers.
  • Use cases include attendance, VIP recognition, blocklist alerts, and secure entry.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you talk to us.

What is Aevrix Visual Intelligence?+
Aevrix Visual Intelligence is an enterprise AI platform that lets you connect live camera feeds, video archives or image datasets and ask questions about them in plain English. The system returns instant answers, timestamped evidence and configurable real-time alerts — without manual review.
Does my video data leave my server?+
No. All visual processing runs on your own infrastructure. Raw images and video frames never leave your server. Only short text descriptions of detected content are used to answer queries, making the platform GDPR-ready and suitable for sensitive industries like healthcare and defence.
Which industries use Aevrix Visual Intelligence?+
Aevrix is used across manufacturing, logistics and warehousing, retail, healthcare, agriculture and smart-city deployments — anywhere there is visual data and questions waiting to be answered.
How fast does the system respond?+
Most queries return answers in under 10 seconds, including evidence clips and timestamps. The system processes 100% of frames across up to 100 concurrent data sources.
Can I integrate it with my existing systems?+
Yes. Aevrix exposes a full REST API plus webhook support and ready-made integrations with Slack and Microsoft Teams. You can embed it into your ERP, build a mobile app on top, or use it standalone.
